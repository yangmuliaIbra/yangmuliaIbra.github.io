As Team Lead, Software Engineer

Digifest 2024 is a prestigious competition festival hosted by SMK Telkom Malang, featuring the Olimawisa (Olimpiade Wikusama) and MIFest (Moklet Islamic
Festival). As the Team Lead and Full-Stack Developer, I oversaw the planning, development, and testing of the web application using the Agile SDLC method.
The platform was built to streamline registration, competition stages, and announcement management, ensuring a seamless experience for both organizers
and participants. Key features included a secure authentication system powered by NextAuth, a robust database with Prisma and MySQL, and efficient data
management through Next.js 14 Server Actions. Upon its launch, the application garnered over 600+ registrations from participants across Indonesia within
its first three weeks, showcasing its scalability and reliability. The project was a collaborative effort with a team of four, and we successfully delivered the
platform within a tight 2-week timeline.
